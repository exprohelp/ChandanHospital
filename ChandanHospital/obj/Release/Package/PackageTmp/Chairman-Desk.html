From the Desk of Chairman

Chandan group is consisting of-

Chandan Healthcare Limited-A Diagnostic company of Chandan group deals with the chain of Diagnostic Centres in the name of Chandan Diagnostic Centre in Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand. These centres are providing complete diagnostic services under one roof including Radiological, Pathological, Cardiac, Respiratory, Gastro and Neurological facilities.

We are proud to have 10 NABL Accreditations at various locations in Uttar Pradesh and Uttarakhand.

The company has established a central referral lab, Chandan Specialty Lab at Biotechnology Park, Lucknow, which is the first of its kind in Uttar Pradesh. The laboratory is a combination of state-of-the-art equipment, global best practices and an extensive intellectual capital and will make Chandan a front-runner in the area of pathology lab services. It is a central referral lab of the company and caters the northern part of India for special tests through its referral centres.

Chandan Pharmacy Limited-A wholesale and retail Pharmaceutical Company of Chandan group deals with wholesale distribution of medicines to its retail pharmacy and Hospital and the chain of retail outlets in the name of Chandan Pharmacy in Uttar Pradesh Uttarakhand and Delhi.

Chandan Hospital Limited-300 bedded Multi-Super-specialty Hospital at Lucknow started on 1st.October,2018 is one of the top three hospitals in the region to serve the population of Uttar Pradesh. Chandan Hospital is serving the patients through all 40 faculties under one roof.

Chandan Institute of Medical Sciences Limited-A medical Educational Institute,1000 bedded Multi-Super-specialty Teaching Hospital and Medical College at Lucknow is starting soon. 11 acre land for the project has been acquired and construction is started. The hospital is proposed to start within two years and it will be followed by Medical College in the same premises.

Chandan has achieved several milestones during the year and has become one of the fastest growing healthcare service provider in Northern India.

Chandan has decided to start another 300 bedded Multi-Superspecialty Hospital at Haldwani(Uttarakhand) in next two years.

Our unique business model, a combination of Diagnostic services and Retail Pharmacy at the same premises will remain the focus area in the near future.

The strict quality control ensures a high level of efficiency in all the segments of Chandan Group.

Our Healthcare industry is growing at a rate of 15% annually, and we have achieved a commendable growth of more than 20% annually. The industry needs a minimum of 25% growth to meet the healthcare requirements of our country and fill the prevailing gap in healthcare services. Our expansion plans in the Hospitals, Pharmacy and Diagnostic services are bound to contribute to the healthcare system.

The real strength of the company lies in its people as they are the most valuable asset we have. My belief in our people, their performance, integrity and a strong system of values, standards and quality will make Chandan as one of the most admired groups in the healthcare industry.
